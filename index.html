What is vegan diet?

A vegan diet contains only plants (such as vegetables, grains, nuts and fruits) and foods made from plants. Vegans do not eat foods that come from animals, including dairy products and eggs.

My favorite snack

  • 2 cups oats
  • 1 cup almonds
  • 2 tbsp unsweetend coco powder
  • 20 pitted dates (soak them in water for a few hours)
  1. Blend almonds and scoop out 1/3 on a large plate.
  2. Add oats and coco powder to your blender and blend it all into a fine flour like consistency.
  3. Drain the dates and add them to the blender. Blend with slow speed.
  4. Scoop out a little bit of the mixture and make small balls which you can roll in the almond powder you previously left aside on a plate.

That is it! Now you can leave them on the plate and store in the fridge for days.

Tofu with sesame oil

  • Tofu
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Agave syrup
  • Air fryer
  1. Cut toffu in cubes.
  2. Mix sesame oil, soy sauce and agave syrup and dump toffu in the mixture. Let it sit for a few hours.
  3. Drain tofu and cook in air fryer till it is crispy.

I like to steam some veggies and cook the rice or quinoa to make it a complete meal.

Better than Minestrone soup

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Grape tomatoes
  • Green beans
  • Yellow onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices: salt, garlic powder, all-spice, pepper, dried parsley
  • Crockpot is a bonus!
  1. Cut and saute onions in the crockpot
  2. Cut carrots, potatoes, green beans and add them to the crockpot. You can cook it on a high pressure for 6min.
  3. Cut tomatoes and add to crockpot with spices, tomato sauce and cook for 2h on "slow cook" mode.

This soup is nutritious and filling. I like adding red pepper flakes or pickled jalapenos to my bowl :)

Fast and yummy burritos

  • 1 cup kidney beans
  • half a cup frozen corn
  • 3 small peppers
  • tortillas
  • vegan mayo
  • nutritional yeast
  • spices: cilantro, garlic, salt, red pepper flakes, all-spice
  1. Soak overnight and cook beans seperatly
  2. Saute peppers and corn till it softens a little
  3. Add cooked beans and spices to your liking. Mix and cook for 5 more minutes.
  4. Spread mayo on tortilla, sprinkle 1 tbl nutritional yeast. Add beans mixture and some arugula (if you like it).
  5. Roll up the burritos and cook on low with a lid on for 3-5min each side.

Tip: you can roll the burritos and keep them in air-thight container for the next day.
