Vegan choice

What is vegan diet?

A vegan diet contains only plants (such as vegetables, grains, nuts and fruits) and foods made from plants. Vegans do not eat foods that come from animals, including dairy products and eggs.

My favorite snack

  • 2 cups oats
  • 1 cup almonds
  • 2 tbsp unsweetend coco powder
  • 20 pitted dates (soak them in water for a few hours)
  1. Blend almonds and scoop out 1/3 on a large plate.
  2. Add oats and coco powder to your blender and blend it all into a fine flour like consistency.
  3. Drain the dates and add them to the blender. Blend with slow speed.
  4. Scoop out a little bit of the mixture and make small balls which you can roll in the almond powder you previously left aside on a plate.

That is it! Now you can leave them on the plate and store in the fridge for days.

Rice with peanuts

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 3 scallions
  • 3 small red peppers
  • 1 cup unsalted raw peanuts
  • soy sauce
  • garlic powder
  1. Cook the rice separately
  2. Chop scallions and peppers into small pieces
  3. Saute till it softens a little bit
  4. Sprinkle garlic powder
  5. Add the peanuts and stir occassionally adding soy sauce
  6. When the peanuts are rosted, add the rice and stir everything adding soy sauce and salt to your liking

Now you could add some fresh salad as a side or steam some broccoli and you have yourself a yummy asian style meal.
